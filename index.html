Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How can I book a flight?

+

You can book a flight through our website by selecting your departure and destination cities, choosing your travel dates, and completing the booking process with your personal details and payment information.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. For cancellations within 24 hours, cancellation fees may apply.

Can I select my seat during booking?

+

Yes! Seat selection is complimentary for all passengers. You can choose your preferred seat from our interactive seat map during the booking process.

What documents do I need for domestic flights?

+

For domestic flights within India, you need a valid government-issued photo ID such as Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID card.

What is included in the ticket price?

+

Our ticket prices include seat selection, meals, beverages, in-flight entertainment, and 20kg checked baggage allowance. No hidden fees!

How early should I arrive at the airport?

+

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights to allow sufficient time for check-in and security procedures.
